About Us

MiB

Make in BVB (MiB) is the echo of infinite innovative ideas framed to restructure the country. With "Make in India", "Start Up India, Stand Up India" as the driving power, the journey of MiB in restructuring the future of the country accelerates. This unique body strives for the revolutionary evolution of entrepreneurial thinking through the envisioned entrepreneurial ecosystem. Every action of it aims at the profound development of persona of the society. To be precise "Inspiring minds to innovate".



Alumni

The proud alumni of MiB, who have evolved through the different strata of technological and entrepreneurial ecosystem are set out to actuate the device that drives the growth of society. They have laid a firm foundation on which this student body is growing exponentially. The firm framework that they have framed privileges us to frame them in this frame of success.

office Bearers

The pioneers who invoke the path of entrepreneurial bliss by evoking the embedded spirit in every individual through the execution of energetic events. These bunch of active minds work at the grass root level to realize the mission and vision of MiB. Ably strengthened by the guidance of CTIE, they showcase the brilliance in team work. They make themselves available round the clock for MiB.

Success Story

The effectiveness of any body or organization is explicit by its success. The determined work of MiB has made the success to be deterministic. Though the story of success is never ending, the snaps portrayed here do communicate the joy of success. The huge success in events, and transformation of the mindset of students have validated the success.
